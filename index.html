About Us

ACHOR is a Community-Based Organization (CBO) formed in 2018. It is legally registered with the Relief and Rehabilitation Commission of South Sudan. Our mission is to provide services to those in need, promote unity, and improve the quality of life for communities through education, health, and sustainable development.

ACHOR has multi-skilled people who are committed to providing Humanitarian assistance such as Education, Health and Agricultural practices.

Moto

Transforming community lives.

Vision

An empowered south Sudanese community operating to it's full potential in all aspects of human life in just Fair equitable society.

Mission

Empowering the community with necessary skills that enable them to achieve their target.
