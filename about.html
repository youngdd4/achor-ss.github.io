About Us

ACHOR is a Community-Based Organization (CBO) formed in 2018. It is legally registered with the Relief and Rehabilitation Commission of South Sudan. Our mission is to provide services to those in need, promote unity, and improve the quality of life for communities through education, health, and sustainable development.

Objectives

1. To promote education ,health and agricultural growth skills among the rural community for a better future.

2. We are destined to support the needy rural communities with adequate care child protection, rehabilitation support with innovative service initiative to alleviate the lives economically socially culturally and health wise.

3. Empowering the community with necessary skills that enable them to achieve their target.

4. To facilities the communities to become self sufficient and self reliant by contributing to education, health and agriculture for sustainable development.
